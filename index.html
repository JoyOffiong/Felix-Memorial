Felix Amaefule

(1942 - 2022)

Prince Felix Chukwuemeka Ochieze Amaefule was born to the Illustrious family of Thomsmith Amaefule Osuala on January 2nd 1942. He was the second child of the family of many sons and daughters as His father had 5 wives. Until his death, Prince Felix Amaefule was the head of the Amaefule family after receiving the mantle following the passing away of his elder brother Nnanna Anthony Amaefule in 2013. Born much in the pre-independence era Prince Felix lived at a time under the British rule.
